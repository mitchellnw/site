Mitchell Wortsman

I am a PhD student at the University of Washington co-advised by Ali Farhadi and Ludwig Schmidt. I am interested in understanding and improving deep learning. My research so far has studied neural network fine-tuning, robustness, sub-networks, and subspaces.

Previously, I spent a great year as a member of the PRIOR Group at the Allen Institute for AI (AI2) with Roozbeh Mottaghi, and as a research intern at Apple with Mohammad Rastegari.

I completed my undergraduate degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at Brown University in Providence, RI. For my senior honors thesis I was advised by Kavita Ramanan. During undergrad I completed internships at Facebook (advised by Yannis Kalantidis) & Microsoft.

I am originally from Toronto, Canada and enjoy music, skiing, hiking, camping, reading, traveling, and climbing.
